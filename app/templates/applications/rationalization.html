Canonical Disposition Taxonomy

— governed 7R action vocabulary used across all rationalization views

The TIME framework (Tolerate / Invest / Migrate / Eliminate) drives the health scoring model. Each score maps to a canonical 7R action that EA practitioners communicate to stakeholders. The mapping is: TOLERATE → Retain, INVEST → Refactor, MIGRATE → Rehost / Replatform / Replace (resolved by infrastructure signals), ELIMINATE → Consolidate (when sibling app overlap detected) or Retire.

Retain

Keep as-is — adequate fit, minimal risk

↑ TOLERATE

Rehost

Lift and shift to new infrastructure

↑ MIGRATE

Replatform

Move to new platform with minor modifications

↑ MIGRATE

Refactor

Re-architect for quality or strategic fit

↑ INVEST

Replace

Swap for a different product or build

↑ MIGRATE

Consolidate

Merge with overlapping applications

↑ ELIMINATE

Retire

Decommission — no replacement needed

↑ ELIMINATE
