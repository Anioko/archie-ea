How it works

  1. A Teams meeting ends — Microsoft Graph fires a callRecord change notification to the Notification URL above.
  2. A.R.C.H.I.E. fetches the meeting transcript via Graph API (OnlineMeetingTranscript.Read.All).
  3. The AI Architect analyses the transcript against the live portfolio (850+ apps) and extracts: architectural decisions, application names, risk mentions, and follow-up actions.
  4. Findings are pushed as in-app notifications to enterprise architects and platform admins — no polling required.

Note: Teams meeting transcription must be enabled for the tenant, and the meeting organiser must have transcription turned on during the call for a transcript to be available.
